POST-PROJECT CARE

Aftercare & Customer Service

One-Year Workmanship Correction (Callback) Period

After your project is completed, we stand behind our workmanship for one year. If something we installed isn't performing as intended, we return to correct it—calmly, promptly, and with minimal disruption.

Q1
What's covered?
  • Corrections to our workmanship (fit, finish, installation quality).
  • Adjustments that naturally settle after use (door alignment, cabinet reveals, caulk touch-ups).
  • Warranty support coordination for covered items we installed.
Q2
What's not covered?
  • Normal wear and tear, owner-caused damage, or lack of maintenance.
  • Issues tied to manufacturer defects (handled under the product's own warranty; we'll help coordinate).
  • Changes in scope or new work requests (quoted separately).
  • Environmental movement beyond industry tolerances (e.g., seasonal wood movement, building settlement).
Q3
How to request service?
  • Email or call with a brief description, photos/video, and your availability.
  • We acknowledge within 1 business day and schedule a visit if needed.
  • A tech or project lead assesses on site and performs the correction or orders parts.
  • If a manufacturer claim applies, we assist with documentation and coordinate next steps.
Q4
Typical response & scheduling
  • Assessment visit: 3–7 business days (availability may vary).
  • Minor adjustments: Often resolved during the visit.
  • Parts-dependent items: Scheduled upon arrival of materials.
Q5
Manufacturer warranties
  • Many products carry their own warranties (tile membranes, fixtures, appliances, flooring).
  • We register/record details where applicable and assist you in initiating claims.
  • Decisions and timelines are set by the manufacturer. We keep you informed.
