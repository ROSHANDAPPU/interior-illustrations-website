Project Story

This contemporary living room transformation created a sophisticated yet comfortable space for a growing family. The clients wanted a room that could seamlessly transition from daily family activities to elegant entertaining.

Our design emphasized clean lines, neutral tones, and flexible furniture arrangements. We incorporated built-in storage solutions and carefully selected lighting to create zones within the open-concept space while maintaining visual flow.

The result is a versatile living area that perfectly balances style and functionality, serving as the heart of family life while impressing guests with its refined aesthetic.

Project Details

  • Location: Stamford, CT
  • Style: Contemporary Modern
  • Budget: $75,000
  • Timeline: 2 Months
  • Team Size: 4 Professionals
  • Furniture: West Elm & CB2
  • Lighting: Philips Hue Smart System
